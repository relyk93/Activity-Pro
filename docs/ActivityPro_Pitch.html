The Challenge

Activity Directors Are Overwhelmed — and Residents Pay the Price

Senior living activity programs are vital to resident wellbeing, but the people running them are buried in administrative work, fragmented tools, and manual processes that would be unrecognizable in any other healthcare setting.

Activity directors in skilled nursing, assisted living, and memory care facilities carry enormous responsibility: planning meaningful programming for dozens of residents with complex needs, tracking participation for regulatory compliance, communicating with families, and maintaining clinical documentation — all with tools that were designed for a different era.

40%+

Time Lost to Paperwork

Activity directors spend nearly half their working hours on administrative tasks — scheduling, documentation, and compliance reporting — instead of spending that time with the residents they serve. Every hour on paperwork is an hour away from the floor.

📋

No Unified Toolset

Most facilities piece together calendars in Excel, session notes on paper forms, family communication through personal email, and compliance records in binders. There is no single system of record. Information lives everywhere, which means it is effectively accessible nowhere.

⚠️

Compliance Is a Manual Risk

Regulatory bodies require documented evidence of participation, individualized programming, and resident engagement. These reports are assembled by hand, often at survey time, from whatever paper records survived the quarter. Inconsistency is inevitable — and inspections are unforgiving.

🚪

Knowledge Walks Out the Door

Senior living has among the highest staff turnover rates in healthcare. When an experienced activity director leaves, they take everything with them: which residents respond to music therapy, who tends to decline in winter, which activities drive the highest engagement. There is no institutional memory.

The Solution

One Platform. Every Tool an Activity Director Needs.

Key Features

Designed for the Realities of Senior Living

Every feature in ActivityPro was built to solve a specific, recurring pain point that activity directors face. No bloat, no generic SaaS tooling — every screen earns its place.

🤖

AI Activity Generator

Powered by Anthropic's Claude AI, the generator produces a full week of personalized, disability-aware activities in seconds. It considers your resident population's mobility limitations, cognitive levels, sensory needs, and documented preferences to create programming that is both engaging and clinically appropriate.

Key benefit: Hours of planning reduced to a single click
🌅

Smart Daily Briefing

Every morning, ActivityPro surfaces an AI-synthesized briefing for the shift: residents with declining mood trends, anyone who has not attended an activity in 5+ days, today's sessions and expected attendees, and any flags requiring director attention. Staff arrive informed, not scrambling.

Key benefit: Critical resident changes surface automatically
📋

Session Pre-Brief

Before any activity begins, staff can pull up a pre-brief for that specific session: every attending resident's relevant disabilities and accommodations, their mood trend over the past two weeks, and their full history with this particular activity type. Preparation that used to take 30 minutes takes 30 seconds.

Key benefit: Staff arrive prepared for each resident's individual needs
🃏

Resident Quick Cards

A compact, scannable profile for every resident: 19 standardized disability types (mobility, vision, hearing, cognitive, behavioral, and more), engagement rate over the trailing 30 days, time since last documented activity, and current mood baseline. Built for fast reference during handoff or pre-session review.

Key benefit: Any staff member can understand any resident in under 60 seconds
✉️

One-Button Family Updates

ActivityPro drafts a personalized, warm, professionally written family update email using AI, pulling from the resident's recent sessions, mood data, and participation highlights. The director reviews and sends with a single click. No more composing individual emails or templating by hand.

Key benefit: Family communication that used to take an hour takes minutes
📈

Mood & Engagement Analytics

Before-and-after mood tracking for every session reveals which activities consistently lift resident wellbeing and which fall flat. Trend charts show individual and cohort-level mood over time. A day-of-week heatmap identifies when engagement peaks and when programming should be adjusted.

Key benefit: Data that demonstrates clinical value to surveyors and families
📄

Clinical PDF Reports

Generate care plan-ready documentation with a single click: participation summaries, mood trend reports, engagement analytics, and individualized resident activity histories. These are true PDF downloads, formatted for clinical binders and compliant with standard documentation requirements.

Key benefit: Survey-ready documentation without manual assembly
📷

Photo Documentation

Attach photos to any session record to build a visual participation history. Photos are stored securely, linked to the session and attending residents, and can be included in family updates or printed in reports. A picture of a resident engaged in an activity is worth more than any checkbox.

Key benefit: Visual evidence of engagement for families and compliance
🔐

Staff Logins & Roles

A two-tier role system distinguishes between directors (full access, including analytics, reports, and settings) and floor staff (session documentation and resident lookup). Every action is timestamped and attributed, creating an audit trail that demonstrates accountability and supports accreditation reviews.

Key benefit: Appropriate access controls with full accountability
