Session Pre-Brief

Resident Quick Cards

One-tap access to everything about a resident: conditions, interests, staff notes, mood trend, engagement rate, and days since their last activity. Flag at-risk residents instantly.

Mood & Engagement Trends

Visual charts show resident mood before and after each session, engagement by day of week, and your most effective activities — ranked by real data, not guesswork.

One-Button Family Updates

ActivityPro writes the family email for you. Add a contact email per resident, click preview, click send. Families stay informed without adding an hour to your day.

Smart Daily Briefing

Photo Documentation

Attach photos to any session directly from your phone or tablet. Build a visual record of activity participation that families love and regulators respect.

Clinical PDF Reports

Generate care plan-ready resident reports as true PDFs — no browser print dialog. Includes engagement history, mood data, disability profile, and a care plan summary paragraph.

How It Works

Up and running in minutes.

No training required. No IT department needed. If you can use a phone, you can use ActivityPro.

1

Add Your Residents

Enter each resident's name, room, disabilities, dietary needs, and interests. ActivityPro uses this to tailor every activity recommendation to your specific group.

2

Generate Your Calendar

Click "Generate Week" and the AI creates a full schedule of disability-appropriate, interest-matched activities for your residents. Edit anything you like.

3

Run Sessions & Track

Use the pre-brief before each session. After, log attendance and mood in seconds. ActivityPro builds your reports, alerts, and family updates automatically.
